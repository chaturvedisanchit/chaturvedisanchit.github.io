Welcome to my webpage! I am a fourth year PhD student at Stanford University advised by Jonathan Luk.

Previously, I was an undergraduate at Courant Institute for Mathematical Sciences (NYU). As an undergrad I worked in Plasma Physics with Antoine Cerfon and in Stochastic homogenization with Scott Armstrong.

I am broadly interested in nonlinear partial differential equations especially those arising from physics. Recently, my work has been focused on questions coming from Kinetic theory and compressible fluids. A goal for the near future is to learn General Relativity and explore its interactions with Kinetic theory.
